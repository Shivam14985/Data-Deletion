Account & Data Deletion

AgriAspire Coaching respects your privacy and provides options to delete your account and associated data

Delete from inside the app

  1. 1Open the AgriAspire Coaching app
  2. 2Go to Menu → Delete my account and data → Select Yes
  3. 3Confirm your request

We'll permanently delete your account and all associated data from our systems.

Request via email

If you can't access the app, email us at bhardwajshivam667@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your in-app User ID (if available)
  • The name associated with your account
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
Send Deletion Request

What we delete

When you request account deletion, we remove the following information:

  • Account profile (name, email, phone number)
  • App activity (quiz history, progress, leaderboard entries)
  • User-generated content (messages, comments, uploads)
  • Payment information (except records required for financial compliance)
  • Device information and usage data

Important information

Data Retention: Some data may be retained if required by law (e.g., fraud prevention, tax, security purposes). We'll explain any exceptions if they apply.

Processing Time: We aim to complete all deletion requests within 30 days of verification.

Reactivation: Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered. You would need to create a new account if you wish to use our services again.
