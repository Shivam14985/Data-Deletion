Account & Data Deletion

Delete from inside the app

  1. Open the app > Click Menu > Click Delete my account & data.
  2. Confirm the request. We’ll permanently delete your account and data.

If you can’t access the app

Email us at bhardwajshivam667@gmail.com with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your in-app User ID (UID)

What we delete

  • Account profile (name, email)
  • App activity (quiz history, leaderboard entries)
  • User-generated content (messages, uploads), where applicable

Retention: Some data may be retained if required by law (e.g., fraud prevention, tax, security). We’ll explain any exceptions if they apply.

Timeline: We aim to complete deletion within 30 days of a verified request.
